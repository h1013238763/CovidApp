About Hummingbird

Hummingbird provides a fast way of assessing the risk of Covid-19. Users can input their symptoms, history, and other factors to receive an assessment of the likelihood of having COVID-19. Hummingbird also provides an assessment of infection severity and offers information on getting officially tested and treated. We focus on using information from the greater Los Angeles area.
