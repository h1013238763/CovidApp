Symptoms of Covid

COVID-19 symptoms can range from mild to severe and may include fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste or smell, fatigue, and body aches.

  • Fever or Chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

The following list only shows a number of symptoms. It is important to keep in mind that people with underlying conditions like heart or lung disease are at a higher risk for getting very sick from COVID-19.

When to Call 911

Signs to Watch with a severe case of COVID 19

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • New confusion
  • Inability to wake or stay awake
  • Pale, gray or blue-colored skin, lips, or nail beds, depending on skin tone

Again, if you are experiencing any of these symptoms, call 911 immediately.
